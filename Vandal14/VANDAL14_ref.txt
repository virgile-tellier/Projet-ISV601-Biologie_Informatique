CC   VANDAL14 is an autonomous DNA transposon. It generates
CC   9 bp duplications of AT-rich target sites upon integration
CC   of its copies in the genome.
CC   There are 5 copies of VANDAL14, 100% identical to each other,
CC   we found in chromosomes I,III,IV and V. These copies are
CC   flanked by 9 bp duplications of different target sites.
CC   VANDAL14 encodes three putative proteins.
